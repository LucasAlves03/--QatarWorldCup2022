Informations

THE 2022 FIFA World Cup or 2022 FIFA World Cup will be the twenty-second edition of this sporting event, an international tournament of soccer male organized by International Football Federation ( FIFA ), which will occur in the Qatar. The 2022 edition will be the first held in the Middle East and the last to have the format of 32 teams, since the competition will have a change in the format and number of teams in the edition of 2026, where it will be based in the Canada, United States and Mexico, moving to 48 teams.

With seven host cities, the championship will be played between November 20 and December 18, instead of the traditional grace period at high temperatures that the country suffers in the middle of the year; this is the first time that a world cup will be played at the end of the year.

Accusations of corruption were made after Qatar won the right to host the championship. FIFA carried out an internal investigation into these allegations and acquitted Qatar of any crime committed. On May 27, 2015, Swiss federal prosecutors opened an investigation into corruption and money laundering in the elections of the host cities of the editions of 2018 and 2022.

Qatar decided to submit its application only for 2022. With only 1.6 million inhabitants, the country claimed to be the first nation in the Middle East to receive such competition, trying to bring the western and eastern worlds closer together.

Qatar suffered several criticisms about the conditions of workers in the new stadiums for the competition, with the Amnesty International referred to how slave labor the conditions of workers, who were abused by human rights, violating several rules of the institution.

THE FIFA presented the official mascot of the 2022 World Cup. It is about La'eeb , a nice traditional male headscarf from Arab culture. According to FIFA, the word La’eeb means skilled player in Arabic.

One of the concerns of the application was the very high temperatures that are registered in that country. The World Cup was always held between June and July, exactly the period of recess of the European leagues. During this period of time, temperatures easily exceed 40 degrees celsius, never dropping to less than 30 degrees celsius.O sheik Mohammed bin Hamad bin Khalifa al-Thani, authority responsible for the 2022 candidacy, stated that the introduction of a technology inside the stadiums is underway in order to be able to lower temperatures by about 20 degrees Celsius.

On June 7, 2015, it was announced that Qatar could lose the right to host the championship after complaints of bribery. According to Domenico Scala, representative of the FIFA audit, the complaints arose after the result of the vote of election of the host city was released. However, no evidence has been proven and Qatar has been confirmed as the headquarters of this edition.
